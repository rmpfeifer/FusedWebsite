Rules for Fused game servers

If you encounter someone breaking any of these rules,
join our discord and submit their SteamID and evidence in the #report-a-player channel

These rules are universal across any Fused servers.

  1. No being a dick. This is our most important and most strict rule. Ruining another player's experience is unacceptable, and the bans will be longer.

  2. No cheating / exploiting. You will get permabanned, and appeals will not be granted for cheats.

  3. An Admin's say is final. If you have a problem with one of our admins, DM Kolyma on discord.

These rules are for our squad servers.

General

  1. No racisim, or any other disrespectful language.
  2. No sharing intel with the enemy team.
  3. ALL squad leaders must have SL kit.
  4. ALL squad leaders must have a mic.
  5. Excessive teamkilling, sabotauge, or any other related acts will earn a ban.

Rules for Vics

  1. First come, first serve for small things like logis.
  2. Vics that require a crewman kit ALWAYS need 2+ people. Ignoring this rule is a fast ban.
  3. A squad named 'Tank' has priority for a tank over a squad named 'Armor'.
